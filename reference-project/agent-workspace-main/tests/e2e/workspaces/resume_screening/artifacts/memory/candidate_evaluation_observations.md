# Candidate Evaluation Observations - Causally Hiring Round 1

## Key Insights for Future Reference

### Company Profile: Causally (广州可溯理科技有限公司)
- **Type**: AI-native app incubation studio
- **Mission**: Incubate 10+ AI-native products/year with small team
- **Philosophy**: AI as default productivity unit, not just efficiency tool
- **Work Style**: "Vibe coding", result-oriented, fast iteration cycles
- **Target Candidates**: "超级个体" (super individuals) who collaborate closely with AI
- **Location**: Guangzhou, China
- **Funding**: Completed 10M+ RMB financing, 5-year runway

### Position Requirements Summary

#### 1. 软件工程师（架构/全栈方向）
**Critical Success Factors:**
- Must demonstrate actual use of AI tools for coding (LangChain, LLM APIs, etc.)
- Product ownership mindset > pure technical execution
- Ability to ship from 0-1 quickly
- English reading ability for tech docs
- Experience level varies by tier (0-1yr, 1-2yr, 2-5yr)

**Red Flags:**
- No evidence of AI tool usage in development workflow
- Traditional enterprise development background without startup experience
- Location mismatch (Guangzhou required)
- Overqualified candidates may not fit culture

#### 2. 传播与体验负责人（设计/前端/增长方向）
**Critical Success Factors:**
- MUST have shipped frontend code (not just Figma designs)
- Portfolio must show live products/pages
- AI tool proficiency for creative work
- Growth/marketing data-driven mindset
- Prefers 0-2 years experience (un固化 thinking)

**Red Flags:**
- Design-only background without frontend skills
- Salary expectations significantly above budget (9-14K range)
- Senior designers (5+ years) may be too expensive and culturally misaligned

#### 3. 办公室助理（行政/人事/运营支持）
**Critical Success Factors:**
- Detail-oriented, reliable personality
- Willingness to learn AI office tools
- Basic English reading
- Startup environment adaptability

## Candidate Evaluation Patterns Observed

### What Makes a Strong Match:
1. **AI-first mindset**: Candidates who already use AI tools daily for their work
2. **Shipped products**: Evidence of end-to-end delivery, not just components
3. **Quantifiable impact**: Numbers showing efficiency gains, user growth, etc.
4. **Startup agility**: Small team experience or strong personal projects
5. **Right experience level**: Not overqualified for the role's scope

### Common Dealbreakers:
1. **Location mismatch**: Guangzhou is mandatory for on-site collaboration
2. **No AI tool usage**: Traditional development/design workflows don't fit
3. **Salary misalignment**: Expectations far above budget ranges
4. **Overqualification**: Senior candidates may resist the fast-scratch culture
5. **Design-only portfolios**: No evidence of shipping code/live products
