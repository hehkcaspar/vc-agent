# WeBox Monitoring Observations

## Company Overview
- **Name:** WeBox (DBA of Saltalk Inc.)
- **CEO:** Fred Ming
- **Business Model:** Intelligent marketplace for food delivery/catering with hub-based operations and SaaS platform
- **Investment Status:** USER is an investor; company seeking Series B funding

## Key Investment Highlights

### Growth Trajectory
- Consistent ~20% quarterly revenue growth maintained throughout 2025
- Q1-Q3 2025 YTD Revenue: $17M
- YoY growth rates: 90-112% across quarters
- Successfully pivoted from dire situation (6 months runway left in early 2024) to sustainable growth

### Market Position
- Competitors actively studying business model (one filed lawsuit - validation of value creation)
- Strong customer acquisition: 38 → 42 active customers over 2025
- Major enterprise wins: Google Fiber, Lightmatter, NIO, Fiserv, etc.
- Diversifying revenue streams beyond recurring SaaS (Group Catering +210%, Office Pantry +34%)

### Operational Scale
- Hub expansion: Santa Clara → SF Bay Area (SF, San Carlos, Mountain View, Sunnyvale) → Fremont Kitchen
- Team growth: 71 → 94 employees (32.4% increase)
- Geographic presence: US (SF Bay Area), China (Shanghai, Wuhan), Philippines

### Fundraising Progress
- **Series B:** Officially kicked off June 2025
- **Due Diligence Stage:** John Paulson Banking & Alloy Technology Partners in advanced stages
- **Upcoming:** Capstone Partners on-site due diligence
- **VC Pipeline:** HongShan Capital (Sequoia China), Ground Force Capital, iFly.vc
- **Bridge Financing:** Already secured $3M loan from Bridge Bank (Q1 2025)

## Critical Metrics to Monitor

### Financial Health
1. **Net Profit Trend:** 
   - Q1: -$116K → Q2: -$46K → Q3: -$332K (significant miss vs $20K forecast)
   - Q4 goal: $20K profit
   - **Concern:** Cost overruns in legal (+$70K), payroll (+$207K), location upgrades (+$88K), fraud (+$49K)

2. **Gross Margin Pressure:**
   - Q1: 30.37% → Q2: 30.17% → Q3: 28.47%
   - Deliberate aggressive pricing strategy impacting margins

3. **Revenue Quality:**
   - WeBox Mart growing rapidly (+38% QoQ in Q3)
   - New BD revenue streams showing strong momentum

### Operational Risks
1. **Customer Concentration:** Lost Archer (major customer) in Q1 - lesson learned on relationship management
2. **Legal Exposure:** Ongoing lawsuit from competitor
3. **Fraud Issues:** $49K fraud cost in Q3 indicates need for better controls
4. **Hub Utilization:** Track occupancy rates as expansion continues

### Strategic Initiatives
1. **Positioning System:** Implemented Q2 2025 - critical for performance management
2. **OKR Alignment:** Company-wide system driving accountability
3. **AI Integration:** Being leveraged for productivity gains (Q3+)
4. **Culture Building:** Core values anchoring hiring and retention

## Red Flags / Areas of Concern

1. **Q3 Net Profit Miss:** $352K variance from forecast is significant
2. **Margin Compression:** Aggressive pricing may not be sustainable long-term
3. **Cost Inflation:** Payroll up $207K QoQ needs justification
4. **Fundraising Timeline:** No update in Q4 letter on Series B progress
5. **Competitive Threat:** Lawsuit indicates market attention but also risk

## Positive Indicators

1. **Execution Discipline:** Maintained ~20% quarterly growth despite challenges
2. **Product Evolution:** 2-tier → 3-tier pricing structure shows learning
3. **Operational Excellence:** Delivery issues reduced to "nearly zero" with new workflows
4. **Talent Development:** Promoting from within (CS Manager, Sales Manager)
5. **Market Validation:** Competitor lawsuit = proof of value proposition

## Future Milestones to Watch

2. **Profitability Path:** Q4 target $20K profit; need sustained path to profitability
